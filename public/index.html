Deal of the Month

Broken Heart

Rs. 299

Rs 249

Introducing the "Broken Heart Plant" – a resilient beauty with a story to tell.
Known scientifically as Anthurium andraeanum, this stunning plant features
heart-shaped, glossy leaves with vibrant, long-lasting flowers resembling a
broken heart.

  • Light: Place your plant in bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Water: Keep the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
  • Humidity: Maintain a humid environment by misting the leaves
    regularly or placing a tray of water near the plant.
  • Temperature: Keep your Broken Heart Plant in a warm environment,
    ideally between 65°F to 80°F (18°C to 27°C).
  • Fertilization: Feed your plant with a balanced liquid fertilizer every
    month during the growing season (spring and summer) to encourage
    healthy growth and blooming.
  • Pruning: Remove any dead or yellowing leaves to promote new growth
    and maintain its attractive appearance.

With proper care, your Broken Heart Plant will flourish, filling your space with
love and beauty for years to come.
